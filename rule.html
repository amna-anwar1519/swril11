Basic Rules of Football

  • Each team has 11 players including a goalkeeper.
  • The match lasts 90 minutes (two 45-minute halves).
  • No hands allowed (except the goalkeeper inside the box).
  • Goals are scored by getting the ball into the opposing goal.
  • The team with more goals wins the match.
